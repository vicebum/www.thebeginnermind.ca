*disclaimer

The creator of this website is not a trained practitioner. The Let’s Practice page prompts are based on the Eight Week Mindfulness and Stress Reduction Course for youths. They are also of a Western background and not of a Buddhist, or Eastern religious lineage. If you are seeking trauma-informed meditation and mindfulness resources, it would be advised to speak to a trained professional and not via the resources supplied through this website.

This website was created for the DART 349 final project. November 21st, 2022.
